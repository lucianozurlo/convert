PROJECTS.

Explore our latest projects, where innovation meets execution. From hardware solutions to digital experiences, Convert® Studios brings ideas to life with precision and impact. See how we've helped businesses build, scale, and succeed.

Steadyrack

We collaborated with Steadyrack’s design team to develop a user-centric cover system for their new ProFlex bike storage solution, focusing on easy installation and seamless functionality.

Gymate

Gymmate keeps fitness programs simple while helping users stay motivated, kick their fitness goals, and work out together anytime, anywhere.

Olya

The Olya app is designed to enhance workplace procurement efficiency, empowering team members to manage equipment orders and replacements while ensuring adherence to company policies and budgets.

Foundri

Foundri is an all-in-one online platform that helps entrepreneurs, creators and companies find the right manufacturer in a trusted and secure marketplace to produce their products.

Lightrium

Lightrium is an innovative solar-powered lighting system that captures sunlight during the day and uses it to illuminate your home at night. We contributed to the development of the hardware, creating a structurally robust device that embraces future solar technologies and seamlessly complements any home environment.

Herman Miller

Convert was engaged to create a technology mounting system compatible with Colebrook Bosson Saunders (Herman Miller) monitor arms. We provided end-to-end service, from design and manufacturing to ongoing order management, leveraging our vetted supplier network.

Kyri

We played a key role in the development of the Kyri smart ring, a groundbreaking device that is changing the way women understand their cycle and their body. Our focus was on optimising user experience, form, and manufacturability to successfully bring the product to MVP launch for Kyri.

Ruok

Quentin (RUOK) is a smart device designed to inspire one million conversations, serving as a symbolic tool akin to an Olympic torch, to encourage meaningful discussions about mental health. Convert was involved in the development of Quentin, helping bring this impactful device to life.

Cisco Stand

Cisco smart devices are industry leaders in their field. We collaborated with Cisco to develop peripheral products that enhance the user experience and expand the reach of their technology. Our work included creating various products to complement and promote their digital whiteboards, Webex Board Pros, to a wider market.

Chevron

Chevron's newly constructed headquarters in Perth, Australia, showcased the latest in workplace technologies. Convert was engaged through Diversified to help design and create a Tech Café featuring cutting-edge digital touchscreen LG technology.

ABOUT.

Founded in 2008, Convert® Studios is a multidisciplinary design company specialising in creating innovative products and digital experiences for new and established brands. We also design, develop, and market our own products across various retail and online platforms. This hands-on experience gives us a deep understanding of what it takes to bring marketable products and services to life.

With an entrepreneurial mindset, we don't just design—we think like business owners. We understand the challenges of launching and scaling products, so we align our expertise with your business goals to deliver solutions that don't just meet expectations but exceed them.

Our expertise spans both physical and digital design, with a strong focus on website and app development, as digital transformation and the rise of IoT and connected devices continue to grow. We help businesses build intuitive user experiences, high-converting websites, and powerful digital platforms that keep them ahead in an evolving market.

Our team is based in Australia and Argentina, with a strong network of manufacturing partners across the APAC region, allowing us to seamlessly transition from concept to production.

Over the years, we've collaborated with industry leaders such as Cisco, Herman Miller (Colebrook Bosson Saunders), Woodside, BHP, HPA, Orora, Kyri, Diversified, Steadyrack and RUOK, delivering high-impact solutions across multiple sectors.

At Convert® Studios , we combine innovation, strategy, and execution to create products and experiences that drive success.
